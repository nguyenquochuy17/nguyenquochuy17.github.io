Interested in IT

My interested in IT

Being able to create software that can help people to meet their demands is what I am interested in IT. In my opinion, the feeling of supporting someone by the app that I am building would be the best feeling. Moreover, I also love programming even though I just have started it recently. My interest in IT started when I saw my uncle coding. He is a game developer and seeing how he built his own game was really cool. He is the one that inspires me to choose this major.

Why did you choose RMIT

The reason why I choose to study at RMIT is because I want to learn in an international environment. It helps me to have more opportunities to work and live in other countries as well as seeing many new foreign friends and different cultures. Besides, I believe RMIT has suitable facilities and excellent teachers to serve for my IT career.

What do you expect to learn during your study?

During my studies, I expect to be a good programmer that can code many different types of programming languages and be able to create some apps, games or websites. Also, I want to learn and expand my knowledge of IT as much as possible.
